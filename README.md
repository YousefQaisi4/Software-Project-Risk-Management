# Risk Management in Software Projects: From Initiation to Delivery

## Overview

This research investigates the role of risk management in improving software project outcomes throughout the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC). The study explores common software project risks, risk mitigation techniques, and the impact of structured risk management on project success.

## Research Objectives

* Identify common risks in software development projects.
* Analyze risk management practices across SDLC phases.
* Evaluate risk identification and mitigation techniques.
* Examine the relationship between risk management and project success.
* Provide recommendations for improving software project outcomes.

## Key Topics

* Risk Identification
* Risk Assessment
* Risk Analysis
* Risk Mitigation
* Risk Monitoring and Review
* Agile Risk Management
* AI and Machine Learning for Risk Prediction

## Research Methodology

The study adopts a qualitative research approach based on secondary data collected from academic journals, conference papers, IEEE Xplore, ScienceDirect, and SpringerLink.

## Key Findings

* Early risk identification improves project success rates.
* Continuous monitoring reduces project uncertainty.
* Effective stakeholder communication enhances project stability.
* Agile practices support flexible risk management.
* AI techniques can improve software risk prediction.
